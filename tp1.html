CONTRIBUTIONS TO NATION



Space and Defense Advancements:
Dr. Kalam played a pivotal role in India’s space and defense programs.
He directed the development of India’s first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV) and led projects for ballistic missiles. His expertise in missile technology earned him the title ‘Missile Man.’
Under his guidance, India achieved significant milestones in missile development, bolstering our national security.
Healthcare and Innovation:
Dr. Kalam’s vision extended beyond science and technology. He championed accessible healthcare through innovations like the ‘Kalam-Raju tablet.’
His commitment to rural development led to initiatives like PURA (Provision of Urban Amenities to Rural Areas), bridging gaps between urban and rural communities.

SOCIAL LIFE

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, born on **October 15, 1931** in **Rameswaram, India**, led an extraordinary life marked by humility, dedication, and unwavering commitment to science and education. Hailing from a modest Tamil Muslim family, Kalam's upbringing emphasized education and moral values. His thirst for knowledge led him to graduate in Physics from **St. Joseph's College, Tiruchirapalli**, and later earn a degree in aeronautical engineering from the **Madras Institute of Technology**. Kalam's scientific career included significant contributions to India's missile programs at the **Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO)** and his role as project director of the **SLV-III** at the **Indian Space Research Organisation**. His achievements earned him the nickname "Missile Man." Serving as the **11th President of India** from **2002 to 2007**, Kalam's accessibility to people, especially students, left an indelible mark. His legacy extends beyond science, as seen in his **Technology Vision 2020**, aimed at transforming India into a developed society. On **July 27, 2015**, at the age of **83**, he collapsed while interacting with students in **Shillong**, leaving behind an enduring impact. Dr. Kalam's remarkable journey exemplifies resilience, passion, and a commitment to nation-building.
